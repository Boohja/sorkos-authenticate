Data privacy

Sorkos Login stores only the information needed to identify you, show your profile correctly, and keep your login flow comfortable across Sorkos projects.

What we store

We store as little data as possible for the login service to work.

  • Email address Used to identify your account and support email-based login.
  • Display name Shown inside Sorkos projects where a name is useful.
  • Avatar link Stored as a link when a provider supplies one, so projects can show your profile image.
  • Preferred language Used to show login screens and messages in the right language.

Remove your data

If you wish to delete your data, find the "Manage Sorkos account" link on any Sorkos project once you are logged in.

This will take you to the account area where deletion and account controls belong.
